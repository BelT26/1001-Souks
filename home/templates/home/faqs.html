Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my items take to arrive?

Due to the individual nature of the items, please allow 3-4 weeks for delivery to the UK. Personalised and bespoke orders will take 4-6 weeks.

If you require delivery outside of the UK please get in touch via our contact form.

What if I change my mind and want to cancel my order before it arrives?

All orders can be cancelled up to 14 days from delivery. If you need to cancel later please contact us via our contact form.

My item arrived damaged. What should I do?

Please send us a photo of the damaged product. We will arrange a full refund or a replacement.

I would like to return or exchange a product. What should I do?

Please send your details on the contact form including your order number and we will be touch to discuss.
